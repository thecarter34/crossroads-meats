Built to Order · Starting at $25

Gift Baskets in Spooner, WI

Wisconsin cheese, house-made jerky and snack sticks, and curated bottles — built to order for every occasion.

Need a gift that tastes like northern Wisconsin? We build custom gift baskets starting at $25 — Wisconsin cheese, house-made jerky and snack sticks, and curated bottles from our cellar. Tell us the occasion and we'll put one together.

What Goes in the Basket

  • Wisconsin cheese — sharp cheddar, cheese curds, gouda, jack
  • House-made snack sticks (cheese-filled and classic)
  • House-made whole-muscle jerky
  • Sausage — fresh brats or dried, your pick
  • Curated wine and craft beer (six-packs available)
  • Locally-made pantry goods — jams, mustards, honey

Occasions We Build For

  • Holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, Easter, Mother's & Father's Day)
  • Thank-you gifts for clients, contractors, and teachers
  • Corporate gifting — single or multi-recipient, with custom notes
  • Housewarmings, weddings, and grad parties
  • Cabin weekend — drop one off on your way through Spooner

How to Order

Stop in, call (715) 416-0989, or message us on Facebook with the occasion, the recipient's tastes, and your budget. We'll put a basket together and have it ready for pickup (or curbside) — usually within a day.

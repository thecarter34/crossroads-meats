CRN Meats & Cellar is a family-owned butcher and specialty food store in Spooner, Wisconsin — right on Hwy 63 between Minneapolis and Duluth. We select our cuts from our sister facility in Amery, age them 30 days, and make more than 30 bratwurst flavors in-house. Stock the freezer with our bulk beef and pork packages, build a custom gift basket, or order a party tray for the weekend. Curbside pickup available. Whether you're stocking up for the cabin, planning a weekend grill session, or hunting for the perfect Wisconsin cheese and wine pairing, you'll find it here.

Family-Owned & Hands-On

A family-owned butcher and cellar, rooted in Spooner.

Crossroads of the North Meats and Cellar is the work of the Lutz family — a family-owned shop built for a town they love. Spooner sits at the crossroads of Wisconsin's railroad history, lake country, and working farms, and that heritage runs through everything we do: hand-cut meat, house-made brats, and a curated cellar picked with care.

We're putting the finishing touches on 118 Ash Street, right in the heart of downtown. Our plan is simple — bring back the kind of local butcher and corner store where you know who's behind the counter, where the brats are made in-house, and where the cheese and wine were chosen by someone who actually tried them.

The Lutz family is committed to being hands-on partners with their team and their community. They can't wait to open the doors and welcome you in.

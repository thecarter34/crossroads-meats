Wild Game Processing

Deer & Wild Game Processing in Spooner

Drop off your deer — we'll handle the rest.

Washburn County is some of the best deer hunting in Wisconsin, and we're your local processor. Drop your deer at 118 Ash Street in Spooner and we'll turn it into the cuts you want — steaks, roasts, sausage, snack sticks, jerky, or ground venison.

How it works

  1. Field dress your deer and bring it in — call ahead during November, we get busy
  2. Tell us how you want it cut (we have a standard cut sheet, or bring your own)
  3. We'll process it (typically 3–5 days during season)
  4. Pick up vacuum-sealed and frozen, labeled by cut
Call ahead during November. Gun deer season opens mid-November — that's our highest-volume period. A quick call to confirm capacity and timing saves everyone frustration.

What we make from your venison

  • Steaks (backstrap, loin, sirloin)
  • Roasts (shoulder, leg)
  • Ground venison / burger
  • Sausage (breakfast, Italian, brat, jalapeño cheddar — your choice)
  • Snack sticks (variety of flavors, cheese-filled available)
  • Jerky (whole-muscle, your seasoning or ours)

Pricing

Processing is priced per hanging pound. Sausage, snack stick, and jerky batches are an additional charge per pound finished. Bring us your trim and we'll turn it into brats, snack sticks, or jerky — a great way to use the whole animal. Ask us for current season pricing.

Other wild game

We also process bear, elk, and other game on request — call ahead to confirm.

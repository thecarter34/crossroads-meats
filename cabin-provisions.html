Cabin Provisions in Spooner, WI

Stock up for the weekend — hand-selected steaks from our sister facility in Amery, Wisconsin cheese, craft beer, and house-made brats. Right on Hwy 63.

Heading up to the cabin? Crossroads of the North is your one-stop shop on the way through Spooner — just off Hwy 63 between Minneapolis and Duluth. Skip the grocery store cooler and load up on real butcher-cut steaks, Wisconsin cheese, house-made brats, and a six-pack of craft beer for the cooler.

What to Grab for the Cabin

  • Hand-cut steaks for the grill — ribeye, sirloin, NY strip, filet
  • 30-day dry-aged options for a special night
  • House-made brats — 30+ flavors (classic beer brat, cheddar jalapeño, wild game)
  • Wisconsin cheese board — sharp cheddar, cheese curds, gouda, jack
  • Snack sticks and jerky for the boat, deer stand, or porch
  • Craft beer (six-pack to go) and curated wine

Why Stop in Spooner

  • Spooner is roughly the halfway point between Minneapolis and Duluth on Hwy 63 — about a 2.5-hour drive from each.
  • We're at 118 Ash Street, two blocks off the highway, with easy parking.
  • Most cabin-goers are in and out in 15 minutes.

Travel Tip

Pre-order by message on Facebook if you've got a long drive planned — we'll have it bagged and ready when you pull in. Curbside pickup is available — call (715) 416-0989 when you arrive and we'll bring your order out.
